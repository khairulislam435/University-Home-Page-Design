Victory Day 2018 at AUB

Asian University of Bangladesh observed 48th Victory Day on 16 December 2018. It reminded us sacrifices of millions for liberty of a nation from occupation forces after a nine-month long bloodstained war for liberation. Honorable Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Abul Hasan M. Sadeq was the Chief Guest in this Program. The Program was chaired by Registrar Dr. Md. Shah Alam.

Fresher Reception and Prize Giving Ceremony

Fresher Reception of Fall 2018 and Prize Giving Ceremony of Intra department Football Tournament 2018 were held on 3 Dec 2018 at Ashulia Campus. Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Abul Hasan M. Sadeq was the Chief Guest and Md. Faruq Ahmed, Additional Registrar, AUB was the Chair in these programs.

Closing Ceremony and Cultural program of BBA 53rd batch

Fresher Reception of Fall 2018 and Prize Giving Ceremony of Intra department Football Tournament 2018 were held on 3 Dec 2018 at Ashulia Campus. Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Abul Hasan M. Sadeq was the Chief Guest and Md. Faruq Ahmed, Additional Registrar, AUB was the Chair in these programs.

With the Citizens of Assam and Burma

Honorable Vice Chancellor Prof. Dr. Abul Hasan M. Sadeq met a group of visitors in the field Inside the Red Fort of Delhi, who came from Assam and Burma, and shared ideas on regional issues. Finally, our VC sir spoke about racial harmony and mutual responsibilities to each other.The citizens of Assam and Burma raised their hands to show that they promised not to harm others.
